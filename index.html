Homemade Chapati

Ingredients:

  1. Flour
  2. Water
  3. Salt
  4. Oil

Steps:Sift the flour and salt into a large bowl. Sprinkle over the oil, and add enough water to make a soft dough, add a little more flour or water if needed. Knead gently until smooth. Cover and leave to rest for about 30 minutes. Roll and cook on pan. Add oil as needed

Perfect Ugali

Ingredients:

  1. Maize flour
  2. Water

Steps:Bring water to a boil in a saucepan, then gradually stir in maize flour until the mixture thickens and forms a firm dough-like consistency. Keep stirring to avoid lumps and ensure even cooking. Once it's smooth and pulls away from the sides of the pan, let it steam for a few minutes, then shape and serve hot.

Ugali is best enjoyed with vegetables, meat, or stew for a hearty, traditional Kenyan meal.
